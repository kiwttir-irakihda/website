Automated Car Parking Assist

Course Project: Autonomous Cyber Physical Systems, USC

Nowadays we dread taking our car to the mall or a plaza. Searching for a parking spot is a big hassle. Then after shopping , you'd need to carry the bags back to your car. In a usual situation it would be easier to hire a valet to do the job for you. As a part of the curriculum we developed an automated valet system. We designed maps using a google earth and created KML files. Given the map, we designed a route plan using RRT. Given the route plan, we designed a kinematic plan use Dubins and Ackermann Wheel's Model. The motion plan was locally controlled using a PID controller. This plan was tested for safety criticality and liveliness using STLs using Breach on Simulink.

Soccer Video Analytics

Course Project: Computer Vision, PES University

During a soccer match, it may be necessary for players to understand their performance in terms of passing, and goal accuracy. Usually in the real match we are provided with multiple camera views, with their intrensic and extrensics parameters. With these parameters, it is easy to know the exact position of each player and the ball. Unfortunately, we do have a inexpensive dataset for these feeds. The objective of our project was to use the video from a soccer match live feed and determine the player and ball position and make some analytics. We were able to create a virtual map of the field and estimate where each player is, who has the ball and which team does the player belong to.

Space Settlement Design Competition

Competition: Bishop Cotton Boys' School

The Space Settlement Design Competition is hosted by NASA and the Atlantic Space Research. It is based on high school students designing a habitable settlement orbitting around the earth. For this competition I led the Human Factors team. As a member of the team, we had to design solutions for the housing and daily life of humans who will be a part of the settlement.The team came 2nd in the Asia round and 5th worldwide in the competition.
